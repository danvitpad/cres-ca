## 5 Patterns That Separate Elite from Average

### 1. Trust Architecture is Non-Negotiable
Top performers layer multiple trust signals: background checks, satisfaction guarantees, insurance partnerships, review systems. Average sites mention "trusted pros" without proof.

### 2. Specific Impact Stats Beat Vague Claims
TaskRabbit's "3.4 million furniture assemblies" crushes generic "thousands of happy customers." Concrete, large numbers tied to specific outcomes.

### 3. Action-Specific CTAs Convert Better
"Book Plumbing Help" outperforms "Get Started." Elite sites use service-specific CTAs that reduce decision friction.

### 4. Emotional Headlines + Rational Subheads
Custodia's "Stop Worrying About Home Maintenance, Start Living" (emotion) paired with rational details is the winning formula. Average sites lead with feature lists.

### 5. Persona-Driven Content Architecture
Custodia segments by persona (Seniors, Busy Professionals, Families). HomeStars segments by location and trade. Average sites use one-size-fits-all messaging.

## Recommended Design Direction for CRES-CA

### Color Palette

| Role | Hex | Rationale |
|------|-----|-----------|
| **Primary** | `#1a56db` (strong blue) | Trust, professionalism |
| **Secondary** | `#059669` (emerald green) | Growth, reliability |
| **Accent** | `#f59e0b` (amber) | Urgency, warmth — for CTAs |
| **Dark** | `#1e293b` (slate-900) | Text, headers |
| **Light BG** | `#f8fafc` (slate-50) | Clean backgrounds |
| **Alt**: Keep `#6366f1` as accent — stands out from every competitor |

### Typography
- **Keep Geist** — modern, clean, distinctive. No competitor uses it.
- Add serif for headlines if warmth needed (Playfair Display or DM Serif Display)

### Content Architecture
1. **Homepage**: Emotional headline + service grid + trust bar + testimonials + CTA
2. **Service Pages**: One per service (Handyman, Cleaning, Plumbing, Electrical, HVAC) with pricing, process, FAQ
3. **Location Pages**: City-specific (Toronto, Vancouver, Montreal, Calgary, Ottawa) for SEO
4. **About Page**: Team story, licensing, insurance, guarantees
5. **Reviews Page**: Aggregated with Trustpilot/Google integration
6. **Blog**: How-to content for SEO

### CTA Strategy
- Primary: **"Get a Free Quote"** (consultation-first, low friction)
- Secondary: **"Book [Service Name]"** (action-specific on service pages)
- Tertiary: **"Call Now: [Phone]"** (always visible in header)

### Key Differentiators to Exploit
1. **"Universal" positioning** — No competitor owns "all home services under one roof" in Canada
2. **Bilingual (EN/FR)** — Mandatory for Canada, no competitor does this well
3. **Commercial + Residential** — Most focus on residential only
4. **Subscription model** — Custodia proves it works
5. **Modern tech stack** — Next.js/Vercel gives speed and SEO advantages
