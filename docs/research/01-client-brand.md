## Tone of Voice

The current copy is **professional-casual, SaaS-oriented**, written entirely in Ukrainian. It uses clean, benefit-driven copy with short sentences. The tone is modern tech-startup: "Book your favorite master in one click." It emphasizes ease, convenience, and transparency.

---

## Key Messaging

- **Headline**: "Book your favorite master in one click"
- **Value Prop**: Convenient service for clients and professionals. Book online, manage schedules, track everything in one place.
- **Social Proof (claimed)**: 10K+ users, 50K+ bookings, 99.9% uptime, 4.9 average rating
- **Pricing**: Starter $12/mo, Pro $29/mo (popular), Business $49/mo. Free for clients.

---

## Critical Observation

The current website is a **Ukrainian-language SaaS/CRM booking platform** — NOT a home services company website targeting Canadian consumers. The entire site needs to be repositioned if the goal is to present CRES-CA as a universal home services company in Canada serving residential and commercial clients.
